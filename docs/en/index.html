Frequently asked questions.

FAQ · Before Seoul
Can I join if I have been away from Ingress for a while, or if this is my first Anomaly?

Yes. We are preparing basic guidance for first-time agents and returning agents. On the day, you will usually move with a team rather than figuring everything out alone.

Are official registration and ENL faction registration different?

They usually serve different purposes. Official registration confirms event participation with Niantic, while ENL registration helps the faction arrange team assignment and operation guidance. We will link the confirmed forms here.

Can I participate if I do not know Seoul well?

Yes. Guidance will be based around subway and walking routes. We will also prepare basic travel information for agents visiting from overseas or other parts of Korea.

What should I bring on the day?

Bring a charged phone, power bank, comfortable shoes, water, and heat protection. Seoul in August can be hot and humid, so prepare for long outdoor movement.

Can I also join Mission Day?

Seoul Mission Day is scheduled for Sunday, August 23, 2026. We will update this page once official details are available.
